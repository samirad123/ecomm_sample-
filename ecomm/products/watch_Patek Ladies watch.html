7300/1450R - TWENTY~4 SELF-WINDING

Rose gold. Sapphire crystal case back. Fully paved with diamondsHaute Joaillerie model

AN HAUTE JOAILLERIE VERSION FOR THE LATEST LADIES’ WATCH.Patek Philippe’s latest ladies’ watch, the Twenty~4 Automatic, now presents its timeless elegance in a rose gold Haute Joaillerie model that celebrates the beauty of the iconic design.
